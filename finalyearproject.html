I want to take the opportunity of my final year project to expand my knowledge and to produce a piece of machine learning software that I can add to my portfolio.

I have chosen neural networks as it’s an area of machine learning I have little experience in, however it has a similar goal to the logistic regression algorithm I have worked with in industry, meaning I already have a good foundation of knowledge on which to build.
